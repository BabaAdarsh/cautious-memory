About Me

I am a postdoctoral fellow at Queen's University, working with Dr. Stephen H. Scott. My research explores how the brain controls and learns movement, using tools like EMG, psychophysics, and computational modeling. Previously, I completed my PhD at IIT Gandhinagar with Dr. Pratik Mutha.

Research Interests

  • Motor control and learning
  • Neurophysiology of movement
  • Computational modeling
  • Markerless motion capture and biomechanics
